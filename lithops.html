    Lithops

    Lithops aucampiae

    Often called "living stones," Lithops are fascinating succulents that resemble small, colorful pebbles. These unique plants have evolved to blend in with their surroundings in the arid regions of southern Africa, making them masters of camouflage. Lithops require minimal water and thrive in well-draining soil, making them ideal for small indoor pots or rock gardens. With the right care, they produce stunning, daisy-like flowers in autumn. Their compact size and intriguing appearance make them perfect for collectors or anyone looking to add a touch of natural artistry to their home decor.
